Featured Destinations

Triund Hill

A scenic ridge in the Dhauladhar range, perfect for beginners and known for its panoramic views of the Kangra valley and snow-capped peaks.

Indrahar Pass

A thrilling trek to 4,342m, offering dramatic landscapes, rocky trails, and a gateway between Kangra and Chamba valleys.

Kareri Lake

A pristine glacial lake surrounded by alpine meadows and pine forests, ideal for nature lovers and photographers.

Laka Glacier

A unique trek to a glacier close to McLeod Ganj, offering a mix of snow adventure and breathtaking Himalayan vistas.

Why HPS Trek?

The HPS (Himalayan Peak Stretch) Trek is not just a hike – it’s a journey through soul-touching landscapes, glacial lakes, and awe-inspiring ridges. Whether you’re a seasoned trekker or a nature lover, this trek offers an unforgettable experience guided by experts with local insight.

Trek Overview

Here’s what you need to know before you trek:

  • ⛰️ Duration: 7 Days / 6 Nights
  • 🚩 Max Altitude: 4,500m
  • 📍 Starting Point: Dharamshala
  • 🕐 Best Season: May – October
  • 👣 Difficulty: Moderate
